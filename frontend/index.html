Academic Excellence

🎓 Pathways to Brilliance

From early years to grade 6, our progressive curriculum sparks lifelong curiosity.

Wonder Years Program

Play-based, inquiry-driven foundation with Montessori influences. Focus on social skills, early literacy, numeracy, and creative expression.

  • Low teacher-student ratio (1:6)
  • Forest Fridays nature immersion
  • Multisensory phonics approach
  • Social-emotional curriculum

Explorers Core

Integrated curriculum merging math, language arts, science, and global studies with project-based learning.

  • Design Thinking challenges
  • Spanish & Mandarin options
  • MakerSpace integration

Leadership Academy

Advanced critical thinking, entrepreneurship, community action projects, and personalized learning pathways.

  • Student council & leadership
  • Capstone innovation projects
  • Advanced STEAM electives

Beyond Boundaries

  • 30+ extracurriculars
  • International cultural exchange
  • Annual innovation fair
